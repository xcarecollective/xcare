    • In the boundless expanse where pixels dance, and we intertwine, our challenge is to infuse every expanded hue pixel with the essence of care [Ndikung & Horelli, 2020] and love, recognizing that the requisites for love [Gilligan, 2011] and the requisites for citizenship in a democratic society are indeed one and the same. Care refers to experimentation of developing methods for rehabilitation, restoration, and restitution through a non-selfish spirit that transcends individual interests and fosters genuine concern for the well-being of all individuals and communities. Because in the time of revolt [Di Cesare, 2021], instead of accepting the internal conflict, we must question the very contours of this space as “we are part of the problem, we must become part of the solution by [collectively] redefining the type of world that ours is becoming.” [Braidotti, 2019]. With a xpeculative vision, we will try to provide a vision on global solidarity [Davis, 2016] by gathering, holding, and carrying - actions that represent nurture, inclusion, and cooperation for a fiction that could embrace complexities of everyday life and diverse human eXperiences [Le Guin, 1986/2020].

    • Aesthetic interaction: We harness the power of aesthetic interaction to create immersive experiences that transcend the boundaries of traditional communication. These experiences encompass sensory exploration, cultural enrichment, creativity, emotional well-being, cognitive stimulation, environmental awareness, inspiration for innovation, and community building through sharing aesthetic experiences.

    • Magic circle of connection: We create a magical circle of connection where individuals come together to share experiences, foster mutual support, and build meaningful relationships. This magical space serves as a haven for collective exploration and the cultivation of empathy and self-care dynamics.

    • Promoting empathy: We aim to promote empathy and genuine concern within our community, recognizing the importance of nurturing individual and collective well-being.

    • Building community interactions: We prioritize creating interactions within our community, promoting collaboration and co-creation between members. By fostering a sense of belonging and inclusion, we empower individuals to contribute their unique perspectives and talents to collective endeavours.

    • Connecting players in collective experiences: We facilitate connections between players, allowing them to participate in collective experiences that transcend the boundaries of individual play. Through shared narratives and collaborative challenges, we foster a sense of unity and mutual support among participants.
